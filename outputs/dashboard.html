Customer Segment Analysis
Understanding the 4 uplift-based customer archetypes
✓ Persuadable
—
Buy BECAUSE of promotion. Highest ROI when targeted. Uplift score > 0.15
Action: Target first 🎯
~ Moderate Responder
—
Some uplift, but marginal. May respond to lighter promotions. Uplift 0.05–0.15
Action: Light touch 💡
→ Sure Thing / Neutral
—
Would buy anyway. Promo just wastes discount spend. Uplift -0.05 to 0.05
Action: Skip discount ⚠️
✗ Do-Not-Disturb
—
Promotions annoy them. Negative uplift — you actually LOSE revenue. Uplift < -0.05
Action: Do not contact ✗

Key Insight: By targeting only Persuadable customers (top uplift scores), you avoid wasting discounts on the — Sure Thing customers who would have bought anyway, and the — Do-Not-Disturb customers whose purchase probability actually decreases with promotion exposure. This selective targeting can improve campaign ROI by eliminating unnecessary discount spend.
